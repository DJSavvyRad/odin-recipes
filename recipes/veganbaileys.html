Vegan Baileys

Description

I saw Julien Solomita make this once on youtube. It looked really good, so I make it around the holidays now too.

Ingredients

  • 14oz full fat coconut milk, unsweetened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon instant coffee
  • 3/4 cup irish whiskey

Steps

  1. With a blender or immersion blender, blend all the ingredients together.
