Savannah's Sweet Cornbread (with Corn), Gluten-Free and Dairy-Free

Description

I have successfully made this cornbread once, and I've never tried it with no gluten and no dairy, so who knows if this is good.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose gluten-free flour, sifted
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup vegan half and half
  • 1/4 cup dairy-free butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup sweet corn, drained

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Thoroughly grease a 9x9 baking dish.
  2. Sift together flour, cornmeal, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Combine the half and half, eggs, butter, and sugar.
  4. Add the liquid ingredients to the dry ones and mix just until the flour is moistened.
  5. Fold in the corn (donâ€™t overmix the batter).
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
